# 🩺 PulseAI – AI Clinical Co-Pilot

> An AI-powered Clinical Operating System designed to assist physicians with patient monitoring, clinical decision support, radiology summarization, and intelligent healthcare workflows.

Built for the **Next-Gen AI Hackathon 2026**.

---

# 🌍 Overview

Doctors in small clinics and hospitals often manage dozens of patients simultaneously. Keeping track of every patient's medical history, vitals, medications, reports, and timelines can become stressful and time-consuming.

Inspired by the real-world challenges faced in a family-run clinic, **PulseAI** was created to reduce physician workload by bringing patient monitoring, AI reasoning, and clinical decision support into one intelligent dashboard.

PulseAI combines real-time monitoring with AI-powered insights, helping healthcare professionals make faster and more informed decisions.
